Troubleshooting LNP OS on ARM64 Phone

If your device fails to boot correctly or gets stuck in fastboot mode, don’t worry — recovery is quick using the LNP Connect tool.

Repair LNP OS Installation

  1. Ensure your phone is in Fastboot mode.
  2. Connect the phone to your PC using a USB cable.
  3. Open LNP Connect.
  4. Click the "Repair System" button.

LNP Connect will re-flash the necessary partitions, restore missing or corrupted files, and reboot your phone safely.

Still Not Booting?

  • Check if your bootloader is unlocked
  • Try a different USB port or cable
  • Ensure USB Debugging and OEM Unlocking are enabled before initial setup

If issues persist, visit the Community Forum.
