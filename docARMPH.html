Install LNP OS on ARM64 Phone

Installing LNP OS on your ARM64-based Android phone is simple, fast, and fully automated using our companion tool LNP Connect. No need to manually flash images — just follow the steps below.

Requirements

  • ARM64 Android phone with unlocked bootloader
  • Windows, macOS, or Linux PC with USB port
  • LNP Connect tool installed
  • USB cable (data-capable)

Step-by-Step Installation

  1. Power off your phone and boot into Fastboot mode (usually by holding Volume Down + Power).
  2. Connect your phone to your PC via USB.
  3. Download and install LNP Connect from our official tools page.
  4. Launch LNP Connect and wait for your device to be detected.
  5. Click "Install to My Phone".

The installer will automatically flash the LNP OS image and install required components like:

  • Fastboot USB driver
  • Google USB driver (if required)
  • Vendor-specific HALs and firmware

Once installation completes, your phone will automatically reboot.

Click "Finish" on the wizard to complete the setup process.
